Cat-sweeper!

Oh no! Your cats have hidden in the boxes you're trying to unpack. Work through the boxes, but be sure not to disturb your sleeping kitties...

Instructions

Click a square once to mark it as a cat-owned space. Click a second time to clear the square.

Cleared squares will display how many cats they are immediately next to, horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.

The game ends when you click on a mine, or when you have cleared all non-mine squares!
